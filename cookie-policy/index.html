Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how ReBi AI uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit www.rebiai.tech. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve efficiency, and provide information to the website owner. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and pixel tags — this policy covers all of them.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be switched off. They include cookies that remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) during a session.

NamePurposeDuration
theme_prefStores your chosen colour theme (dark or light)Session

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website — which pages are most visited, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the site experience. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

ProviderPurposeDuration
Google Analytics (if enabled)Page views, session data, traffic sourcesUp to 2 years

We only activate analytics cookies where permitted by applicable law (including ePrivacy rules in the EU/UK).

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies remember choices you make so we can provide a more personalised experience. At present, the only preference we store is your theme selection.

Marketing Cookies

We do not currently use any marketing or advertising cookies on this website. If this changes, this policy will be updated and appropriate consent mechanisms put in place.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may load content from third-party services (such as embedded video players or LinkedIn widgets). These services may set their own cookies. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those services directly.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (note: this may break certain website functionality)

Browser-specific guidance:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

You can also opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any changes.
