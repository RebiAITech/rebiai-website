Overview

ReBi Automation Cloud is RebiAI Tech's proprietary platform for intelligent process automation (IPA) — the fusion of classic robotic process automation with AI. Where traditional RPA breaks the moment a process deviates, Automation Cloud understands intent, adapts to exceptions, and keeps running, automating entire workflows end-to-end rather than brittle single steps.

It learns from how your operations actually behave, scales across departments without rebuilding from scratch, and records every action for compliance. Every component of the platform is the proprietary technology of RebiAI Tech, made available exclusively under enterprise license.

Key Capabilities

🔁

RPA + AI Automation

Combine rule-based automation with AI decisioning so bots handle judgment-heavy steps, not just repetitive clicks.

🧠

Adaptive Exception Handling

The platform learns from your operations and resolves edge cases that would stop conventional automation in its tracks.

⚡

Event-Driven Orchestration

Processes trigger the instant a business event occurs across your systems — no batch delays, no manual kick-offs.

📜

Complete Audit Trail

Every automated action is logged and traceable, giving compliance and risk teams a defensible record by default.

How It Works

  • Map. Capture how a process really runs, including the exceptions, using process intelligence.
  • Automate. Combine RPA actions with AI decisioning to automate the full workflow end-to-end.
  • Adapt. The platform learns from outcomes and handles variations without breaking.
  • Govern. Every action is logged to an immutable audit trail for compliance and oversight.

Built for the Enterprise

Automation Cloud is engineered for regulated operations — back-office, finance, compliance, and customer operations — with role-based controls and audit logging built in. It operates on governed data from ReBi Data Fabric, invokes autonomous agents from ReBi Agent Studio, and reaches AI models securely through ReBi AI Gateway.
