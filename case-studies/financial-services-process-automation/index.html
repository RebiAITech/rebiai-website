Intelligent Process Automation for a Regional Bank

A $30B-asset regional bank deployed ReBi Automation Cloud across loan processing, KYC, and compliance reporting — eliminating 60% of manual touchpoints, saving $12M a year, and cutting loan turnaround from 9 days to 2, all within a 9-month enterprise rollout.

At a glance

  • Client profile: Regional commercial bank — ~$30B in assets, 1.2M customers, 450 back-office FTEs across operations, risk, and compliance.
  • Engagement: 9-month phased rollout across back-office operations, function by function.
  • Platforms: ReBi Automation Cloud (intelligent process automation), ReBi AI Gateway (governed document AI & PII handling), ReBi Agent Studio (exception-handling agents).

The Challenge

The bank's back office ran on manual, document-heavy processes that buckled under rising volume and a tightening regulatory load. Loan files, KYC checks, and compliance reports moved through queues of analysts re-keying data between systems. The drag was both cost and risk:

9 days
average loan processing time, with most of it spent waiting in manual queues.
38%
straight-through processing rate — the rest required manual handling.
4%
error rate on manually keyed data, driving rework and audit findings.
5 days
to prepare each periodic compliance report, by hand, under deadline pressure.

What We Built

ReBi AI deployed an intelligent automation layer that reads documents, makes routine decisions, and escalates only true exceptions to humans — with a full audit trail behind every action.

  • Document & decision automation (ReBi Automation Cloud). Automated intake, extraction, validation, and routing across loan origination, KYC onboarding, and regulatory reporting — covering the high-volume processes that consumed the most analyst time.
  • Governed document AI (ReBi AI Gateway). Extracts and classifies data from unstructured documents with automated PII handling, redaction, and an immutable log of every model decision for examiner review.
  • Exception agents (ReBi Agent Studio). Autonomous agents resolve common edge cases — mismatched fields, missing documents, watchlist hits — and hand off only genuine judgment calls to staff.

ReBi AI moves at a different speed — less overhead, more engineering. They held themselves to our business outcomes, not a list of deliverables. We redeployed more than 120 people to work that actually needs human judgment.

Why It Worked

In a regulated bank, automation only scales if examiners can trust it. ReBi AI built governance and auditability into every automated decision, so the bank could expand from a single pilot process to enterprise-wide rollout without re-litigating compliance at each step. Rather than cut headcount, the bank redeployed roughly 120 FTEs from data entry to exception handling, customer service, and risk analysis — turning an efficiency program into a capacity gain.
